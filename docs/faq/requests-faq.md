# Requests – Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about user-submitted requests in Intrigma Scheduler.

---

### Can users submit requests after the deadline?

Yes, but late requests may be denied automatically or excluded from automation.

---

### What's the difference between a time off request and an availability override?

- **Requests** are user preferences.
- **Overrides** are scheduler-enforced constraints and take precedence.

---

### Can I limit how many days off a user can request?

Yes — see [User Limits](../features/requests/user-limits.md).

---

### Are requests automatically approved?

Yes, in the Free Edition. Manual approval is optional.

---

### Do work requests guarantee assignment?

No. Requests influence automation but do not guarantee scheduling.
